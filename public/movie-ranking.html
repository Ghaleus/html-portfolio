The Best Movies According to David

My top 3 movies of all time:

Spirited Away

Spirited Away became an instant favorite from the first time I saw it in the mid 2000s. Its animation is beautiful, the story is simple yet poignant, and the incorporation of Japanese folklore elements, both real and original, appeal to the myth loving side of me. It is easily the best anime film I've seen (though many of Miyazaki's films rank high on my list).

Mary Poppins

This is a movie that I truly believe is a 10/10 perfect movie. The creativity, filmography, acting, music, story, special effects, all work in harmony to create a movie that needs nothing added and nothing removed or changed. It is my favorite live action Disney movie and probably my favorite Julie Andrews movie as well.

Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

This is a tough one because I have many favorite movies and wasn't sure which to put. The decision came down to how often I actually find myself thinking of the LOTR and when I do this movie is what I visualize. Watching not only the Extended Editions, but also the commentary and behind the scenese on how they made this classic fantasy movie gives me chills each time. It's infinitely quoatable (as are most of my favorite movies) and holds up in visuals even now. It defined a genre, reinvigorated fantasy movies as actual money makers and has lasted over 20 years and still one of the big contenders in modern film history.
